People Page

People

A list of some famous people during the space race.

1934 - 1968

Yuri Gagarin

Yuri Gagarin was a soviet astronaut who became the first man in space in 1961 aboard the Vostok 1.

1923 - 1998

Alan B. Shepard, Jr.

Alan Shepard was the first american in space aboard the Freedom 7 spacecraft in 1961.

1930 - 2012

Neil Armstrong

Neil Armstrong was the first person to step on the moon aboard the Apollo 11 spacecraft on July 20, 1969.

1930 - Modern Day

Buzz Aldrin

Buzz Aldrin was the second person to step on the moon aboard the Apollo 11 spacecraft on July 20, 1969.
