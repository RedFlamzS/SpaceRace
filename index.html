Space Race

What Was The Space Race?

The Space Race took place from 1955 - 1975 and was a race between the U.S. and Soviet Union to see who could send a human to space and land on the moon first. The Space Race led to many technical changes and inventions like the LED which we use daily. There was also many politictal changes caused by the space race.
